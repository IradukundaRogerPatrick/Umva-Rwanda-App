You are not alone

1 in 5 Rwandan youth lives with depression, anxiety, or trauma in silence. Umva creates a safe, stigma-free space where your feelings are heard — without judgment.

Trusted peer counsellors

Inclusive for everyone

All content is available in Kinyarwanda audio, English, and Rwandan Sign Language (RSL) — serving every young Rwandan including the deaf community.

Works without internet

Umva works offline. No internet? No problem. Your wellness data and content stay available wherever you are in Rwanda.

Professional Help
If your peer counsellor or check-in suggests you need professional support, Umva connects you directly to mental health services covered by your Mutuelle de Santé card.
💳

Mutuelle de Santé

Mental health services are covered. You do not need to pay out of pocket. Bring your Mutuelle card to any referral facility.

How it works
1
Complete your check-in
Your wellness check-in identifies whether professional support would benefit you. Your answers are private.
2
Talk to your peer counsellor
Your trained peer counsellor discusses your situation and helps you decide if a referral is the right step.
3
Receive your referral note
Umva generates a simple referral note you can show at any Mutuelle-linked health facility or mental health centre.
4
Access professional care
Visit the recommended facility with your Mutuelle card. Your peer counsellor can accompany you if you wish.
